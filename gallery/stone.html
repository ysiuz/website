Stone Does
Not Speak


April - September 2024.

InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Figma.

Advised by: Kelsey Elder.

For 419 years the Basilica of Bom Jesus has stood as an iconic cultural heritage site within the distinct Goan region of India.
My task was to map the church's lesser known stories, and highlight the communities that continue to fight for its survival.
