That's my Voice

Visual Identity

AI voices are everywhere — used to streamline, manipulate, entertain and indulge. Yet, are there more to these vocal mimicries than the prompts we give them?
Over the course of 20 Eamses cards and their fronts and backs, I explore the humanity in all of our voices.
It should be noted though, that my work is only made possible through the crucial reporting done about AI voices by Max Read of the New York Times, Allegra Rosenberg of Business Insider, and Pranshu Verma of the Washington Post [ 1, 2 ].
Their work serves as the structure and inspiration for everything that you see below.
