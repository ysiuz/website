Leave No One Off The Map

Public Service Campaign

Roughly 325,000 businesses, homes, and other establishments lack high-speed internet access in Pennsylvania. The vast majority of these people live within underserved, often overlooked rural communities.
My goal was to propose a potential design system that can speak to rural communities and bolster ongoing efforts to close Pennsylvania's digital divide.
